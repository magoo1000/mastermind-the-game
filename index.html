How to Play:

The Game:

If you choose 'Vs. Player', you will be competing against a friend. First, your friend will create a code of 4 colors to fill in the 4 big empty circles. DON'T PEAK CHEATER. Once all of the circles are filled, and your friend clicks the 'Create Code' button, it is your turn. If you hit 'Vs. Computer', the code will have already been created for you. Now, you have 10 attempts to guess the code your friend or the computer has just made. Remember, the same color can be used more than once. When you fill the big circles with your guess, hit 'Submit', and your attempt will be stored in the column to the right for reference.

Feedback:

Black Dot: Correct color in the correct spot.

White Dot: Correct color in the incorrect spot.

Empty Dot: You're sh!t out of luck and it's the wrong color completely.

Reminder: The feedback dots are in no particular order.

For example, just because you get a black dot in the first circle does not mean that the first circle of your code is correct.
